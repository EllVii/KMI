Built with HELP-style clarity

Clear paths for people who need help and people who want to help.

The structure is inspired by strong nonprofit navigation: people should quickly understand where to request support, where to find resources, and how to give or partner.

KMI keeps that clarity while staying ministry-centered, faith-centered, and community-referral focused.

Current site structure

  • Get Support: intake and first step
  • Ministry Areas: services and future sub-pages
  • Resources: housing, food, counseling, scholarships, referrals
  • Give: donations, partners, volunteers, sponsorships
  • About: team, mission, beliefs, values
  • Contact: general contact and partnership interest
