Resources

Practical resources with a faith-centered foundation.

Bridging the gap between crisis and stability.

KMI works as a dedicated referral agency and community resource center, helping bridge the gap between crisis and stability through compassionate service and trusted connections.

🏠

Housing

We believe everyone deserves a safe, stable, and welcoming place to call home, whether you have served our country or are a cherished member of our local community. As a dedicated resource and referral agency, we are here to walk alongside unhoused individuals and veterans, bridging gaps and opening doors to a brighter, more secure future together.

🥫

Food Distribution

We are deeply concerned by the reality of hunger facing our community. We stand firmly beside our neighbors in need, offering not just fresh, wholesome food, but a network of supportive community relationships to walk with you through every challenge.

🎓

Scholarship Association

The Scholarship Association empowers underserved and marginalized prospective college students to achieve higher education by providing dedicated funding, mentorship, and direct access to KMI’s community resources and referral networks.

🧭

Life Coaching

To empower individuals to identify their personal goals, overcome obstacles, and achieve self-sufficiency. Through structured guidance and actionable goal setting, this service helps participants navigate life transitions, build resilience, and unlock their full potential.

🙏

Biblical Counseling

Provide Christ-centered instruction and direction to couples, individuals, and families looking for the true way of life.
