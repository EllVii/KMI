Resources

Housing support, food distribution, scholarship association, life coaching, biblical counseling, and resource guides.

Practical resources with a faith-centered foundation.

KMI works as a dedicated resource and referral agency, helping bridge the gap between crisis and stability through compassionate service and trusted connections.

🏠

Housing

At KMI, we believe that safe, stable housing is a fundamental right for every individual who has served our country or calls our community home. As a dedicated resource and referral agency, we bridge the gap between crisis and stability for unhoused individuals and homeless veterans.

🥫

Food Distribution

To alleviate food insecurity in the local community by providing equitable access to nutritious groceries while seamlessly connecting individuals to essential social services, housing support, and financial resources.

🎓

Scholarship Association

The Scholarship Association empowers underserved and marginalized prospective college students to achieve higher education by providing dedicated funding, mentorship, and direct access to KMI’s community resources and referral networks.

🧭

Life Coaching

To empower individuals to identify their personal goals, overcome obstacles, and achieve self-sufficiency. Through structured guidance and actionable goal setting, this service helps participants navigate life transitions, build resilience, and unlock their full potential.

🙏

Biblical Counseling

Provide Christ-centered instruction and direction to couples, individuals, and families looking for the true way of life.
