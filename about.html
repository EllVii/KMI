About the mission

About Us

At our core, we exist to love God and serve all people by sharing the life-changing Gospel of Jesus Christ. We provide hope, guidance, and spiritual growth through the truth of Scripture to anyone in need.

Servant Leaders

Brother Marc and Sister Helen have a love for God, service, missionary work, and helping people grow through faith-centered support.

Brother Marc and Sister Helen

Brother Marc and Sister Helen have been married for over seven years. Both have a love for God and serving others. During their personal prayer and meditation time, they both received the calling of missionary work.

In 1994, during a memorable family visit to his grandmothers alongside his two sisters and other loved ones, a special moment was shared with Marc. His grandmother back then shared with him not to be surprised if he were to become a preacher or pastor one day, reflecting a deep, early sense of calling.

Fast forward to today, and that spiritual trajectory has come full circle in a profound way. Marc is currently in the process of completing his doctoral and postgraduate requirements in divinity and theology, beautifully manifesting the very path that was envisioned all those years ago.

Sister Helen

Sister Helen is a native of the Philippines and knows first-hand the challenges of growing up without basic everyday necessities, and without the nurturing love and care of a loving mother, who passed when she was five years of age.

Helen has an extreme passion for praise and worship and is spiritually gifted with a profound love of giving and service. She has always put others first and after the passing of her first husband, she had a spiritual awakening that has revealed to her the importance of having a true relationship by putting God first in all that she does.

Mission

To restore marriages and heal families through Christ-centered counseling by creating a safe, compassionate, and faith-centered space where individuals can openly express and navigate life’s challenges.

Through this process rooted in biblical truth and a Christlike mindset, this ministry equips individuals, couples, and families with practical tools, spiritual wisdom, and emotional support necessary to build strong, healthy, and purpose-driven relationships.

Vision

To educate and train people in biblical truth and principles, incorporating practical tools and spiritual wisdom and equipping others to do the same, thus transforming lives by sharing and demonstrating the love of Jesus Christ through action.
