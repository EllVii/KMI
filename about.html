About the mission

About Us

At our core, we exist to love God and serve all people by sharing the life-changing Gospel of Jesus Christ. We provide hope, guidance, and spiritual growth through the truth of Scripture to anyone in need.

Servant Leaders

Brother Marc and Sister Helen are united by their love for God, their missionary calling, and their commitment to serving others. Through faith-centered support, they seek to encourage spiritual growth, strengthen individuals and families, and help people move toward hope and purpose.

Brother Marc and Sister Helen

Brother Marc and Sister Helen have been married for over seven years. Their shared reverence for God and compassion for humanity have shaped their commitment to serving others. Through prayer and reflection, they each received a calling to missionary work—a calling they now pursue together through Kingdom Missions International.

Brother Marc

Prior to rejoining the military in 1994, Marc experienced a meaningful moment during a family visit with his grandmother, sisters, and other loved ones. His grandmother told him not to be surprised if he became a preacher or pastor one day, reflecting an early sense of the calling upon his life.

Today, that spiritual journey has come full circle. Marc is pursuing a doctorate in divinity and theology, bringing to life a vision that began more than three decades ago.

Sister Helen

Helen’s journey is a powerful reflection of God’s ability to transform sorrow into a life of service. A native of the Philippines, she experienced material hardship early in life and the heartbreaking loss of her mother at the age of five. Through those difficult experiences, she developed an extraordinary passion for worship, generosity, and caring for others.

Following the passing of her first husband, a season of deep grief led Helen into a profound spiritual awakening. Today, she walks in a genuine and deeply personal relationship with God, sharing His love generously with every person she encounters.

Mission

To restore marriages and heal families through Christ-centered counseling by creating a safe, compassionate, and faith-centered space where individuals can openly express and navigate life’s challenges.

Through this process rooted in biblical truth and a Christlike mindset, this ministry equips individuals, couples, and families with practical tools, spiritual wisdom, and emotional support necessary to build strong, healthy, and purpose-driven relationships.

Vision

To educate and train people in biblical truth and principles, incorporating practical tools and spiritual wisdom and equipping others to do the same, thus transforming lives by sharing and demonstrating the love of Jesus Christ through action.
