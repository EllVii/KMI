About the mission

About Us

Kingdom Missions International (KMI) operates as a 508(c)(1)(A) exception, religious order, and educational society. We are dedicated to honoring God by serving humanity, sharing the transformative power of spiritual truth, and offering high-quality education. Through the wisdom of Scripture, we provide hope, guidance, and personal growth to individuals from all walks of life.

Servant Leaders

Marc and Helen are a living testament to faith in action. Anchored by a deep love for God and a missionary heart, they have dedicated their journey to serving the vulnerable and nurturing spiritual growth—walking alongside individuals to help them flourish through Christ-centered support.

Brother Marc and Sister Helen

Marc and Helen have been married for over seven years. Both have a reverence for God, a love for humanity, and a heart for serving others. During their personal prayer and meditation time, they both received the calling of missionary work.

Brother Marc

Prior to rejoining the military in 1994, during a memorable family visit with his grandmother, sisters, and other loved ones, Marc experienced a special moment. His grandmother told him not to be surprised if he became a preacher or pastor one day, reflecting a deep, early sense of calling.

Today, that spiritual trajectory has come full circle. Marc is currently pursuing his doctorate in divinity and theology, bringing to life a vision sparked over thirty-two years ago.

Sister Helen

Helen’s journey is a powerful reflection of God’s ability to turn sorrow into a sanctuary of service. A native of the Philippines, she knew early in life the trials of material lack and the heartbreak of losing her mother at age five. Yet, she allowed those empty spaces to be filled with an extraordinary passion for worship and a profound love for giving.

Always one to put others before herself, a season of deep grief after the passing of her first husband ultimately led to a beautiful spiritual awakening. Today, she walks in the fullness of a genuine, deeply personal relationship with God, pouring out His love onto every soul she encounters.

Mission

Guided by our faith, we empower communities through counseling, education, essential resources, and vital referrals to foster hope, dignity, and self-sufficiency for all.

Vision

To ignite hope and cultivate lasting transformation across our neighborhoods, nations, and the world by building bridges of unconditional love, empathy, and active compassion. We stand united in our shared humanity, dedicated to uplifting every individual, honoring every voice, and healing communities—transcending all boundaries of belief, background, and culture to create a more just, harmonious, and connected world for all.

What We Believe

We believe that being made in the image of God gives us the profound responsibility to serve as His ambassadors. It is our daily charge to live out the truths of His Word through authentic character and compassionate behavior by serving and being there for others.
