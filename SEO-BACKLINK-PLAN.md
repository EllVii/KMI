# Kingdom Missions International SEO and Backlink Plan

## Current canonical website

- https://ellvii.github.io/KMI/
- Replace this URL throughout the site, sitemap, directory profiles, and outreach materials after KMI connects a permanent custom domain.

## Primary search themes

Use these phrases naturally. Do not repeat them unnaturally or hide them in the page.

- Kingdom Missions International
- Christian community resources
- Christian community outreach
- food distribution ministry
- housing referral support
- faith-based education
- biblical counseling resources
- chaplaincy certification
- Christian volunteer opportunities
- international missions support

## Backlink rules

1. Do not buy bulk backlinks or submit the website to automated link networks.
2. Do not trade links with unrelated websites.
3. Use the full organization name or a natural phrase as the link text. Avoid repeating the same exact keyword anchor everywhere.
4. Link each partner to the most relevant KMI page rather than always linking to the homepage.
5. Track the source, target page, contact, outreach date, response, and live-link URL.

## High-priority backlink opportunities

### 1. Existing ministry and community partners

Ask churches, food-distribution partners, educators, referral organizations, volunteer groups, and overseas ministry partners to add KMI to their partner or community-resource pages.

Recommended destination pages:

- Community resources: https://ellvii.github.io/KMI/resources.html
- Education: https://ellvii.github.io/KMI/edu.html
- Outreach and volunteering: https://ellvii.github.io/KMI/outreach.html
- Mission and leadership: https://ellvii.github.io/KMI/about.html

### 2. Ell Vii's Automations portfolio or case-study page

Publish a short client project description on Ell Vii's Automations and link to the KMI website. The description should explain that Ell Vii's Automations developed the website and digital intake experience for KMI.

### 3. Organization profiles and directories

Create or complete accurate profiles only where KMI is eligible. Potential categories include:

- nonprofit and ministry directories
- local community-resource directories
- volunteer opportunity websites
- educational partnership directories
- local business or community associations
- church and faith-based organization directories
- event calendars for public outreach events

Use the same official organization name, website URL, description, email, phone, and location on every profile.

### 4. Event and outreach coverage

For each public food distribution, teaching session, volunteer event, or mission update:

- create a useful event or impact page on KMI
- submit it to legitimate local calendars
- ask participating organizations to link to the event or recap
- provide approved photos, a short description, and the exact KMI URL to partners

### 5. Educational partnerships

When a degree or certification partner is finalized, request a link from the partner's approved schools, affiliates, or community-partner page to KMI's Education page. Do not claim accreditation, recognition, or a partnership until it is documented and approved.

### 6. Digital press kit

Prepare a simple press kit containing:

- KMI mission and organization description
- leadership biographies
- logo and approved outreach photos
- website and contact information
- current programs and service areas
- approved quotes and impact statistics

A complete press kit makes it easier for partners, journalists, and community organizations to mention and link to KMI accurately.

## Monthly backlink workflow

- Week 1: identify five relevant partner or directory opportunities
- Week 2: send personalized outreach to five organizations
- Week 3: publish or update one useful KMI resource, outreach, or education page
- Week 4: verify new links, repair broken listings, and record results

## Measurement

Review monthly:

- Google Search Console indexed pages
- search impressions and clicks
- branded searches for Kingdom Missions International
- referring domains
- backlinks to each KMI page
- broken links and crawl errors
- inquiries originating from partner sites

SEO cannot honestly be guaranteed at 100 percent or guarantee first-place rankings. The goal is complete technical coverage, useful content, consistent organization information, and steady growth in legitimate relevant backlinks.
